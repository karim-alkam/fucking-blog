# Equipment

## Tank
My friend's brother had an unused glass tank 50cm in length, 40cm for height and width, so I stole it from him.
to calculate the tank liters here is the formula $$volume(cm^3)/1000 => 40*40*50/1000 = 80 $$
80 liters makes the process easier because its big it will not be affected that much when something wrong happen it will be stable most of the time.

## SOIL (21/1/2026)
I used 9L Aqua soil, I covered the aquarium land with it, in the front the Hight of the soil about 3-4cm, in the back 6-8cm.
this soil is perfect for aquariums specially planted ones it provides everything for the plants, it lowers ph level, but the it produce alot of ammonia making the aquarium not ready to introduce fishes and shrimps until it be stable.

## Light (21/1/2026)
I find 18W light in the store for a good price I think its good for my aquarium but it doesn't have timer so I do turn it on and off manually at the moment but I'm planning to make a timer for it in the future, my tank is 50cm wide and the light I bought is 45cm wide.

## Decoration (24/1/2026)
I bought spider wood it's not just for the tank to look good it have some befits such as biofilm and tannins I talked about this in this document: [[basic self sustainable aquarium ecosystem]], before putting any decoration you have to make sure the thing is clean and not harmful for the aquarium boiling the thing will remove any harmful bacteria, what I did with the wood is pouring it in water for about 3-4 days also with hot water this help prevent your water from any thing harmful and prevent the water turn into brown water even this is helpful its doesn't look nice

## PLANTS (21/1/2026)
